Our Comprehensive Services

At Heaven Veterinary Clinic, we provide complete care for all your pets' health needs

Primary Checkup

Regular health examinations are essential for early detection of potential issues and maintaining your pet's overall well-being.

  • General health examination - Comprehensive physical assessment
  • Disease identification and advice - Early detection and professional guidance
  • Preventive care consultation - Personalized health maintenance plans
  • Age-appropriate screenings - Tailored to your pet's life stage

Vaccination

Protect your pets from preventable diseases with our comprehensive vaccination programs.

  • Vaccines for dogs, cats, poultry, and livestock - Comprehensive immunization
  • Preventive care and immunization - Protection against common diseases
  • Vaccination schedule management - Personalized timeline for optimal protection
  • Health certificate preparation - For travel and boarding requirements

Diagnostic Services

State-of-the-art diagnostic equipment allows us to quickly identify health issues for prompt treatment.

  • Blood test, urine test, X-ray, ultrasound - Advanced diagnostic capabilities
  • Lab tests for accurate diagnosis - Precise identification of health issues
  • Digital imaging - High-resolution results for better diagnosis
  • Rapid results - Many tests processed in-house for quick turnaround

Surgery

Our modern surgical suite is equipped to handle procedures from routine to complex with the highest safety standards.

  • Routine operations (castration, spaying) - Standard surgical procedures
  • Emergency surgeries (injuries, fractures) - Critical care when needed most
  • Advanced soft tissue surgery - For mass removals and abdominal issues
  • Comprehensive pain management - Ensuring comfort during recovery

Medication & Prescription

Our in-house pharmacy provides convenient access to medications with expert guidance on proper administration.

  • Provision of prescribed medicine - On-site pharmacy for your convenience
  • Long-term treatment advice - Comprehensive care management
  • Compounding services - Custom medications for difficult-to-treat pets
  • Dosage guidance - Clear instructions for medication administration

Emergency Care

We provide immediate attention for urgent health concerns, with extended hours to address critical situations.

  • Immediate treatment for sudden illness or injury - 24/7 emergency services
  • Critical care monitoring - Continuous observation for serious conditions
  • Emergency surgery capabilities - Ready to handle urgent surgical needs
  • Trauma care - Specialized treatment for accident injuries

Nutrition & Wellness Advice

Proper nutrition is fundamental to your pet's health. We offer personalized dietary recommendations.

  • Proper diet and supplement guidance - Tailored nutritional plans
  • Lifestyle and health improvement recommendations - Holistic wellness approach
  • Weight management programs - For overweight or underweight pets
  • Therapeutic diet plans - For pets with specific health conditions

Home Care Guidance

We empower pet owners with knowledge and skills to provide excellent care between veterinary visits.

  • Instructions for administering medicine or injections at home - Clear, easy-to-follow guidance
  • Health records and follow-up support - Continuous care beyond our clinic
  • Wound care instruction - Proper techniques for home care
  • Behavioral advice - Solutions for common behavior problems

Specialized Care

We provide expert care for a wide range of animals, from traditional pets to livestock and exotics.

  • Treatment for livestock, birds, and exotic animals - Expertise across species
  • Reproductive care - Breeding and fertility services
  • Avian and reptile specialty care - Specialized knowledge for exotic pets
  • Large animal services - Farm calls and livestock treatment
